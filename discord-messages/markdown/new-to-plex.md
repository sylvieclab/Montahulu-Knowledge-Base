# üìå How to Pin Montahulu+ Libraries

Make it easier to find Montahulu+ content by pinning our libraries to your sidebar! This keeps our movies and TV shows front and center.

## Why Pin Libraries?

By default, Plex shows all available content, including Plex's own free streaming libraries. Pinning Montahulu+ libraries ensures our shared content appears prominently on your home screen.

## üìç Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open Plex and click the **Home** icon
2. Click **More** at the bottom of the left sidebar
3. Find **Montahulu+** libraries (Movies, TV Shows, etc.)
4. Click the **‚ãØ** (three dots) next to each library
5. Select **Pin** from the menu
6. Repeat for all Montahulu+ libraries you want
7. Click **Pinned** at the top to return home

## üóÇÔ∏è Optional: Unpin Plex's Default Content

- Find Plex's default libraries (Movies & TV, Live TV, etc.)
- Click the **‚ãØ** next to each one and select **Unpin**

*You can always access them from the More menu!*

## ‚ÜïÔ∏è Reorder Your Libraries

1. Click the **‚ãØ** next to any pinned library

# üîí Recommended Privacy Settings

Plex has several privacy features that are enabled by default. Here's how to protect your viewing activity and control what data you share.

## 1Ô∏è‚É£ Disable Activity Sharing ("Discover Together")

**What it does:** Emails your friends lists of what you watched recently

**How to disable:**
1. Go to [app.plex.tv](https://app.plex.tv)
2. Click your profile photo ‚Üí **View Profile** ‚Üí **Edit Profile**
3. Click **Manage who can see your activity**
4. Set all options to **Private** or **Friends Only**

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Disable Watch Status Sync

**What it does:** Syncs your watch progress and ratings across Plex

**How to disable:**
1. Go to [app.plex.tv/desktop/#!/settings/account](https://app.plex.tv/desktop/#!/settings/account)
2. Scroll to **Account Visibility & Activity Sharing** section
3. Find **Sync my watch state and ratings**
4. Click **Update this setting** and disable it

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Manage Email Preferences

**What it does:** Controls what emails Plex sends you

**How to manage:**
1. Go to [plex.tv/email-preferences](https://www.plex.tv/email-preferences/)
2. Uncheck any email types you don't want to receive
3. Save your preferences

## 4Ô∏è‚É£ Disable Playback Data Sharing

**What it does:** Sends your playback data to Plex

**How to disable:**
1. Go to [plex.tv/about/privacy-legal/privacy-preferences](https://www.plex.tv/about/privacy-legal/privacy-preferences/#opd)
2. Find **"Send playback data to Plex"**
3. **Uncheck** the box to opt out

## 5Ô∏è‚É£ Review Your Privacy Rights

**Learn more about your data:**
- [Plex Privacy Policy](https://www.plex.tv/about/privacy-legal/#plex-your-privacy-rights)
- Understand what data Plex collects and your rights

## 6Ô∏è‚É£ U.S. Users: Opt Out of Ad Partners

**What it does:** Stops sharing data with advertising partners

**How to opt out:**
1. Go to [plex.tv/vendors-us](https://www.plex.tv/vendors-us/)
2. Follow the opt-out process

## ‚ÑπÔ∏è Important Notes

- These settings are **optional** but recommended for privacy
- Settings may need to be configured in multiple locations
